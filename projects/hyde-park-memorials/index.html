Hyde Park Dedication and Memorial Trees

A project by Angelo Licastro.

Throughout Hyde Park in Niagara Falls, NY, trees—along with a bench and a garden—stand as living tributes to loved ones. Until now, no single record has brought these memorials together. This project seeks to honor them by doing so. I hope these tributes inspire you as they have inspired me.
